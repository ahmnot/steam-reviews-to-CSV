Within the Backrooms is a fresh take on games based upon the Backrooms. I absolutely love the universe that stemmed from the original Creepypasta, and I honestly think it is on par with the SCP universe. Unfortunately, many of the games featuring the Backrooms follow the same thread of games featuring the SCP universe. An over usage of entities and subpar gameplay. However, this game changes that entirely.

This game creates a sense of unease that captures the true essence of the original Backrooms concept. This doesn't have all the entities that other games of this variety have. It is purely atmospheric unease. You'll never know what you'll see next or what you may encounter. The music in this game further adds to the unease you feel while playing this game. You know nothing should happen to you, but in the back of your mind, the music and the atmosphere make you think, "What if?" That is what makes this game frightening yet beautiful. This feeling of unease lessens more and more as you play through all the endings of the game to eventually fully complete the game and discover all the secrets there is to be found, but this feeling of unease is done very well for the time you are feeling it. It sometimes even reappears when you discover a new secret or discover an ending that strikes a certain chord of fear within you.

The gameplay itself is pretty standard, with some puzzles here and there. It also gets very repetitive to repeat the game from the start to see each ending. However, the simple gameplay and the repetitiveness don't detract from the experience, as the main appeal of this game is the atmosphere that is set up for you to explore rather than the riveting gameplay. You can easily just sit back and explore the Backrooms in their pure liminal glory.

Overall, I believe this to be the best Backrooms-themed game in the market currently. Sure, it may not have the "exhilaration" that other games within this small subgenre may have, but it has charm and character that carries the game to maybe become the face of what a Backrooms-themed game should be like.

Within The Backrooms is not like any other Backrooms game you have ever played. There is no monster roaming the halls, other then a pervasive sense of dread and emptiness. It's best described as a puzzle game, but Within The Backrooms really doesn't fit very well into that category, either. The environments are beautiful if often inexplicably 'wrong', and the music is the right level of unnerving for the game. First play-through will probably take anywhere from 30 min to 2 hours, but the game is so full of secrets, lore, and little things to find that you will keep coming back for more. Highly recommended.
