I bought this PCVR game. War of Being is a single-player VR sci-fi adventure with standard mode playability, based on the music and concept for TesseracT's 2023 full length album.


I bought this on launch and experienced several crashes on launching the game. Luckily, the devs were quick to respond and an update today fixed that issue. However, all is not perfect, I still experienced a crash today when I tried using the main menu. Also, optimization is pretty bad in VR. I was getting anywhere from 40-60 fps on my RTX 3080.

Also, the interactions were pretty basic. When interacting with the control panels to open gates or to open a locked container, you basically see a mouse cursor that you guide along with your motion controllers. That used to be common back in 2017 VR, but it's now 2023 and I hadn't seen one of those type of interactions in a while.

Game is running on the Unreal Engine. You do have have full locomotion, along with smooth turning. There may be other options, but like I said, when I tried using the menu, the game crashed on me. There is button crouch, so you can play the game standing or seated. However, I was a bit too low to the ground when playing seated.

Game is a little difficult to describe. I guess you could call it a musical adventure game. If you're a fan of TesseracT, then I can definitely recommend it for you. The game is in Early Access and the devs seem like they are responsive. Hopefully, game gets patched up. It is budget priced so it's a pretty good deal if you're okay with the current issues.

Rate 6/10.
