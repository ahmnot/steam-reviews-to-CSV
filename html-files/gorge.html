Gorge

Positives:
+ Good idea
+ Liked the Wake up, Caves and Reservoir levels
+ Retro inspiration
+ FOV Adjuster
+ Generally a lot of adjustments can be made
+ Help function with video guide, speed adjustments and skip opportunity
+ The basic parkour stuff is mostly ok
+ Its free

Negatives:
- Quirky, unstable, inconsistent controls and physics
- Unstable, buggy and glitchy in general
- Some levels are just unnecessary hard (you know exactly what to do, but its almost not possible to execute)
- I really disliked most of the Gorge, Dam Primary and Secondary levels because of this (which is half the game)
- Bad, glitchy and heavy duty graphics (looks like cr*p, but demands a good pc to run smoothly)
- Achievements that dosent work
- Some annoying sounds and music
- Very frustrating to play because of the above

Where did this go wrong? It was a really good idea, and I can see what they were trying to do, and sometimes also succeeded. But over half the game is so frustrating to play. Thats because of the difficulty level mixed with all the other problems the game has got with inconsistency, unstableness, bugs and glitches. Sometimes the game just decides to live its own life, and prevents you from moving forward.
I havent been so angry in years because of this. And that says a lot.
Its in alpha stage and still need a whole lot of work.
Go play Frostrunner, Crystal Call or Portal instead of this, and save yourself the frustration.
