I even spent some time (which others might consider strange but I found enjoyable) turning one of the circles on and off repeatedly and just listening to how it interacted with the music. If you're expecting a tough puzzler or a fast-paced adventure, then this won't be your game. If you enjoy taking your time to take in the sights and sounds of this game, you'll be in for a pleasant treat. It only lasts maybe 90 minutes or so, but it's well worth it.
